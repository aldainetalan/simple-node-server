My Top 3 Favorite Films of All-Time

The third on my list is Stoker (2013). I have watched this film multiple times. Of all the psychological/gore films that I have watched, this one is my favorite. The film contains just the right amount of gore scenes and eeriness within its charaters. Also, this film has a very interesting ending.

My top 2 film goes to Mean Girls (2004) . This one is a classic. Many chic-flicks have tried to outdo Mean Girls, but they all failed. The script is just so well-written that the characters and some of the lines will remain to you forever. This film is "so fetch"!

My Top 3 Favorite Songs

The third place goes to Rico Blanco- Your Universe . Rico Blanco is definitely an artist! This song makes me nostalgic every time I listen to it. It just brings back happy high school memories.

My second favorite is Two Door Cinema Club- Undercover Martyn . I instantly fell in love with this song, the first time I listened to it. This song was in my playlist for 3 years which made a record in my playlist history. I recommend everyone to check out the amazing album of TDCC called "Tourist" where this song came from.

My number one favorite is Black Kids- Hurricane Jane. My sister introduced me this song when I was in 4th year highschool. I never liked it at first. But after few plays, this song turned out to be very addicting and beautiful. This is now my favorite song especially at times when I want to be lazy and just chill in bed.
